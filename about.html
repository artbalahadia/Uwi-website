Üwi, Inspired by a true story.

'Uwi' comes from a Tagalog word meaning 'Come home', a word to look forward to by couples who are in long distance relationships. Üwi is inspired by a true story of a long distanced couple - Art and Cza, who's been together since 2010, but was separated when Art moved to the United States in 2016. One thing was certain to the success of their relationship, it was true and constant communication. The goal of this app is to keep lives together, close the gaps of loneliness and bring hopes to those who believe. With Üwi, you will never feel away from love, because Üwi pulls hearts together.

"Distance means so little, when someone means so much"

Art and Cza
